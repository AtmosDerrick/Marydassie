How we train our students

In Marydassie Fashion School, we believe in education through practicals and good working ethics. Students are train in badges, a single badges does not contain more than 10 students. This method is enable tutors have better control over the student population, and also to enable flexibility among tutors and students. we train our students both practically and theoretically in order for students to become experts in their feild of study. We also hold seminars to teach students marketing strategies and entrepreneurship skills to help them build and create their brands in the world of business.
