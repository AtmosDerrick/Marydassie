MARYDASSIE FASHION SCHOOL

ABOUT

Marydassie Fashion School (MFS) is one of Ghana's most promising fashion school who is commited into training individuals into becoming an expert and entrepreneurs through descipline and strong knowledge in business ethics and organization.

Marydassie Fashion school is Affiliated to National Vocational Training Institute (NVTI). We offer certificate course in Fashion Designing, Photography, and I.T. We also provide students with the best modern equipment, condusive environment and well structured hostel facilities.

Marydassie Fashion School believe in academic growth through intensive practicals, therefore we are practical base institution who find delight in training student into gaining full potentials.

Our Mission

Marydassie Fashion School mission is to train the younger generations into establishing their own industries. We also want to inspire the youth into vocational skills and technology.

Our Vision

Marydassie Fashion School vission is to educate the youth on the value of vocational skills. we also have the vision of training students practically and building their critical thinking skills, and confidence level in order to make them incredible enterprenuers.
