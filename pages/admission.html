How to Apply
Admission into Marydassie Fashion School is very simple
Just follow the steps listed below.
ADMISSION FORM IS ONLY GHC 50.00.
  1. Fill the admission request form with your correct details.
  2. Click the button below to submit your request form.
  3. Kindly check your email to access the account number for the payment of the admission form.

    4. Note: Admision form is only GHC 50.00.

  4. Make payment through the provided account number.

    5. Note: Use your name as your reference.

  5. After payment, Your admission form will sent to you through your email address.
  6. Download the admission form.
  7. Fill it with your correct details.
  8. You can either:
    1. Scan and send the filled admission form our email(admission.marydassie@gmail.com).
    2. Or you can bring the hard copy of the filled admission form to the school's admission office.
    9. Thank you for taking a bold step into Marydassie Fashion Schoool.
